About Me

I'm Madumere Stephen — a passionate front-end developer, designer, and creative thinker based in Nigeria. I craft modern, responsive, and bold user interfaces with a focus on clean code and pixel-perfect design. Every project I take on is a chance to express both logic and artistry, and I love turning ambitious ideas into usable digital experiences.

I have a deep interest in design systems, animations, and meaningful web interactions. My style leans toward vibrant, futuristic visuals with scroll effects that make users feel something. From playful personal sites to polished UI projects, I'm constantly pushing for more.

I hold a certificate in Web Development, which marks the beginning of my journey into mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, and building scalable applications. Whether I’m building components in Angular or experimenting with motion design, I focus on both performance and personality.

Outside of code, I’m drawn to football (huge Man City fan), fashion design, storytelling content, and creating unique brands around wild ideas — like Shakespearean football accounts or AI conspiracy shorts. I believe creativity shouldn't be limited to one niche.
